About Kartik Kumar

Kartik Kumar is a passionate artist hailing from roorkee(Uttarakhand). Born with a paintbrush in hand and a vision in his mind, Kartik embarked on his artistic journey at a young age.

Despite being a student pursuing his graduation, Kartik dedicates his time and energy to his true calling - art. His relentless pursuit of excellence and unwavering passion for painting sets him apart in the world of art.

Kartik finds inspiration in the world around him, capturing the beauty of nature, the vibrancy of life, and the intricacies of human emotions through his brushstrokes.

Types of Paintings:

  • Wall Paintings: Kartik specializes in transforming dull walls into captivating masterpieces, infusing life and color into every space he touches.
  • Normal Paintings: His talent extends beyond walls, as he creates mesmerizing paintings on canvas, each telling a unique story and evoking emotions.
  • Contract Paintings: Kartik has undertaken numerous commissioned projects, collaborating with clients to bring their artistic visions to life with his expertise and creativity.

With his distinct style, attention to detail, and a profound understanding of art, Kartik Kumar continues to leave an indelible mark in the world of painting.
